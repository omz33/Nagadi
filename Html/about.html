T. Nagadi Group of Factories

Presided by Shaikh Tarik Mohamed Nagadi, the Group is expanding its activities by increasing production capacity with modern machinery to meet the growing demand for quality concrete products across the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia and the GCC.

The latest major expansion is underway at the Al-Kharj T. Nagadi Industrial Complex in Al-Rifaya Industrial Area, spanning 160,000 m². The complex is designed not only for concrete products, but also houses HDPE and Textile factories—supporting Saudi Vision 2030 through the localization of manufacturing. In addition, a factory has been established in the Tabuk region to serve local demand and support contractors with more economical delivery.

Our expansion roadmap aligns with the Kingdom’s economic development plans and Vision 2030. Over the past years we have consistently delivered quality products, earning approvals from numerous government organizations for project supply.

We operate modern production units across more than 500,000 m²: a 160,000 m² Al-Kharj complex (to which the former 40,000 m² Riyadh plant is being relocated), a 200,000 m² Yanbu plant, and a 45,000 m² Tabuk plant.

Our facilities produce Lined & Non-Lined Reinforced Concrete Pipes (for micro-tunneling and open trench), Box Culverts of multiple sizes, a full range of Sewer & Storm Manholes, Telecommunication & Electrical Manholes & Handholes, and other custom precast elements. A key product line is the Concrete Fence Wall. All products comply with Saudi and international standards.

Our strategic locations enable economical coverage across the Kingdom: Riyadh / Al-Kharj serves the Central & Eastern regions, Yanbu serves the Western region, and Tabuk supports the Northern region.

We are prepared to expand further in line with industry demand, adding more machinery as required.

Certified to ISO since 2002, we maintain rigorous quality systems with regular renewals and upgrades. We are also a member of the Pipe Jacking Association, London (UK).
